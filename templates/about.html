Bird Watch

A Deep Learning / AI based Bird Image Identification System.

The 'Bird Watch' project, created by an amateur photographer and a machine learning enthusiast, is a solution to a simple problem faced by fellow wildlife photographers: a way to identify birds in their photographs.
